VALORANT

Dates: Saturday, August 1st @ 2PM ET

Registration Deadline: Friday, July 31st

Cost: Free

Prize: $25 for First Place

Tournament Format: Single Elimination

Match Format: Bo1, Bo3 Grand Finals

Map Selection:

Best-Of-1

Teams will veto until one map is left. Lower seed vetoes first.

Best-Of-3

Teams will pick one map and the third map will be decided at random from the remaining two. The match starts on the lower seed's map choice.

There is a cap of 32 teams for this tournament.

Approximate Schedule:

2:00 PM | Round 1 Matches

3:00 PM | Round 2 Matches

4:00 PM | Quarterfinal Matches

5:00 PM | Semifinal Matches

6:00 PM | Grandfinals

NOTE: This competition is not affiliated with or sponsored by Riot Games, Inc. or VALORANT Esports.
