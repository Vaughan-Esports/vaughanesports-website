June 2020 Monthly's

Registrations below!

Rainbow Six Siege

Dates: Friday, June 12th - Saturday, June 13th @ 2PM ET each day

Registration Deadline: Thursday, June 11th, 2020

Cost: Free to enter!

Tournament Format:

   4 Teams or Less:

  • Round Robin Group Stage (Friday)
  • Single Elimination Playoffs based on that Seeding (Saturday)
   More than 4 Teams:
  • Single Elimination Bracket iwth 4 teams advancing(Friday)
  • Single Elimination Playoffs based on that Seeding (Saturday)

Match Format:

  • All matches are Best-Of-1
  • Finals are Best-Of-3
  • ESL Pro League Settings and HUD

Map Selection:

   Map Pool:

  • Coastline
  • Villa
  • Oregon
  • Clubhouse
  • Theme Park
  • Kafe Dostoyevsky
  • Consulate
   Format:
  • Both teams alternate bans, T1-T2-T1-T2-T1-T2 until one map is left
  • Lower seed gets first ban and gets to pick starting side (In BO3 loser of the previous match is the lower seed)

Smash Bros Ult. Solos

Dates: Monday June 15th - Wednesday, June 17th @ 2PM ET Each Day

Registration Deadline: Sunday, June 14th

Cost: Free to enter!

Tournament Format: Double Elimination Pool with Bracket Reset

Rules: We are using a modified version of the Naifu Wars Ruleset

Character / Stage Selection: All character selection and stage striking will happen through the designated smash.gg lobbies

VALORANT

Dates: Thursday, June 25 - Saturday, June 27 @ 2PM ET Each Day

Registration Deadline: Wednesday, June 24

Cost: Free to Enter!

Prize: TBD

Tournament Format: Single Elminination

Match Format:

  • All matches are Best-of-1
  • Grand Finals are Best-of-3

Map selection:

   Best-of-1

  • Each team will get to veto one map and a map will be chosen at random from the remaining two.
   Best-of-3
  • Each team will pick one map and the third map will be chosen at random from the remaining two.

There is a cap of 16 teams

We are still working on the rulebook and will make an announcement on discord when it is finalized.

NOTE: This competition is not affiliated with or sponsored by Riot Games, Inc. or VALORANT Esports.
